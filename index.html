Hermanos Macehual is a community service based organization that provides academic and social support to students attending UC Davis. Our homepage provides information about our organization, membership, and philosophy. In addition, we feature information about our annual scholarship fund, as well as past, present, and future activities.

Our Constitution

We, the Chicano/Latino students of the University of California at Davis, formally declare, on this date, December Sixteenth Nineteen Hundred eighty-nine, the establishment of this constitution for our fellowship Hermanos Macehual. This organization shall be created to form a brotherhood uniting the Chicano /Latinos in Davis and to provide assistance, within our constraints, to Chicano/Latinos and underrepresented people in any possible manner. We believe it is imperative that this organization provides community service to all who facilitate a need for it and to advocate the highest available education to all our fellowship brothers.
